In-Class 4

Website & App Design Critique

Go through some websites and find some common web components.

Submission

Show completed activity to professor by end of class.

Due: Wed Sept 27, 2023

Starter Files

open_in_new

Document Template

Form a group of 3-4. Find two websites or two apps that are of the same category. For example: coffee apps (Starbucks & Bridgehead), booking sites (AirBnB & Booking.com). Try and find one site that is higher quality and one that is lower quality. Compare how the two sites/app treat similar design patterns and provide examples of how one if more or less effective than the other. Some themes ideas for sites/apps (feel free to do something else)*:

  • food ordering/restaurant
  • transit/bus schedule
  • hotel/bookings
  • music/video streaming

*Try to pick sites/apps that are an actual interactive app, not just an info dump site.

You well then take your findings and create a single group Google Doc. For each of the following topics, provide screenshots from both apps/sites and a few bullet points summarizing the differences:

  • visual style (consider colour, images, icons, graphics)
  • usability (Can you find things? Is it clear where to click)
  • interactivity (how does the app/site react when you use it?)

Heads Up!

We will be spending the first portion of the class doing the critiques in small groups. Then you group will present a portion of your critiques to the class for further full class discussion.
